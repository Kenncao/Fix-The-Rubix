OLL (Orientation of the Last Layer) solves the top color of the last layer. This is the 3rd step of the CFOP method. It consists of 57 different algorithms but I will only provide 15 that are easy to memorize and execute. You can repeat doing F R U R' U' F' until you get one of the cases provided below.
